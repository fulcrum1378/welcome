This app won't access the password of your Instagram account;
it just saves login cookies; which contain a temperory token used for re-initiating the same login session.
They are created when you log in to your Instagram account using an in-app web browser.

* This app will have access to these private areas of your Instagram account:
  • Your followers and following: in order to catch your Unfollowers and let you Unfollow them back.
  • Your saved posts: in order to list them for you and let you download (and Unsave) them.
  • Your direct messages: in order to export them into your storage.
  • The posts of your friends whose profiles are private: you can view their profiles inside this app and download their content with their permission.
  • Your favourite profiles: in order to exclude them from your Unfollowers list as well as some other exceptions.
  • Follow action: this app lets you follow some people with your own permission under certain circumstances.
* This app also accesses some public features of your Instagram account; like viewing public posts and stories.

This app has no back-end server and we extract NOT A SINGLE PIECE OF INFORMATION from your device.

We do not collect or use any personal data that may be used to identify or contact you.

Everything happens inside your device.
