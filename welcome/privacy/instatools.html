This app won't access the password of your Instagram account;
it just saves login cookies; which contain a temperory token used for re-initiating the same login session.
They are created when you log in to your Instagram account using a web browser.

This app will have access to some public and private features of your Instagram account;

  • Public features include followers and following accounts.
  • Private features include your saved posts and direct messages.
